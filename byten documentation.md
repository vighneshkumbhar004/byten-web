SERVICE 1: LAND SURVEYING
Land Surveying Services
Using cutting-edge DGPS, Total Station, and drone-integrated survey methodologies, we deliver precise land data for planning, legal, and infrastructure purposes.

DGPS - Differential Global Positioning Systems: A highly accurate GPS system used to measure and map large areas of land precisely.

DGPS & Drone Integrated Combined Surveys: Drones integrated with DGPS and handheld sensors provide high-resolution imagery with accurate real coordinates.

Topographic Mapping Works: Gathering detailed data including roads, water bodies, contours, levels, and Digital Elevation Models (DEMs).

Customized Survey Works: Tailored survey processes designed and executed on-site based on client requirements.

Route Alignments & Transmission Line Surveys: Comprehensive surveys for power lines, gas/water pipelines, roads, and railways.

Contour Surveys: Detailed terrain models, Digital Elevation Models (DEMs), and Digital Surface Models (DSMs).

Static DGPS Surveys: Millimeter-level precision critical for precise engineering works.

Private / Government Property Boundary Surveys: Precise delineation of property boundaries for development and legal disputes.

Cadastral Surveys: Facilitating mapping and maintenance of village maps and land records.

Partition Surveys & Land Development: Accurate subdivision and layout marking for development planning.

Solar Project Surveys: Land-use/land-cover mapping, thermal inspection, and feasibility reports.
